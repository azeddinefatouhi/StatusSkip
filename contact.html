Before you write — quick answers

I upgraded to Pro but my limit isn't lifted.
Email billing@statusskip.com with the PayPal email you used and the email you enter in StatusSkip. We'll activate manually within a few hours. Automated activation is in development.
My report email landed in spam.
Ask your manager to add the sender address to their contacts. On the first send from a new address, some spam filters apply extra scrutiny. Subsequent sends are typically delivered cleanly.
I want to delete my data.
Email privacy@statusskip.com with the email address you use on StatusSkip. We'll delete all associated submission logs within 14 days and confirm by reply.
I want to request a refund.
We offer full refunds within 7 days of your first Pro payment. Email billing@statusskip.com with your PayPal transaction ID.
