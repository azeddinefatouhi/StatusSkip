I paid but my Pro access isn't active.
Make sure you entered your StatusSkip email before completing the PayPal payment. If you didn't, use the form above and select "Pro activation issue" — we'll activate you manually within a few hours.

We offer a full refund within 7 days of your first payment. Use the form above and select "Billing / refund".
My report email landed in spam.
Ask your manager to add the sender address to their contacts. After the first send it should land in inbox consistently.
Can I request my data to be deleted?
Yes. Use the form above and select "Privacy / data request". We'll delete all your data within 30 days.
