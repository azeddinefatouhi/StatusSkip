FAQ

Questions

Does this work without a Gmail account?
Yes. The form works from any browser. Email is sent via the StatusSkip backend — your manager replies to your own email address. No account connection needed.
Is my data stored anywhere?
Submission data (name, email addresses, task text) is logged to a private Google Sheet for quota management. It is never sold or shared. See our privacy policy for full details.
Will this land in spam?
Unlikely, but possible on the first send. We recommend your manager adds the sending address to contacts. The email content uses clean HTML with no spammy patterns.
Can I customize the email template?
Pro users get three templates: Standard, Executive Brief (minimal, corporate-friendly), and Freelancer Client (adds project name, replaces "Manager" with "Client").
How does payment work?
Pro is $7/month billed via PayPal. You don't need a credit card — PayPal balance or bank transfer works. After payment, reply to your PayPal receipt with the email you use on StatusSkip and we'll activate Pro within 24 hours. Automated activation coming soon.
What if I have more than 3 tasks?
StatusSkip supports up to 5 tasks per report. You can also add a notes and blockers section for anything extra. Good status reports are concise — 3 to 5 tasks is the sweet spot.
