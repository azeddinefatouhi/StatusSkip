Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

The short version: We collect only what we need to send your report. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with third parties. You can ask us to delete it at any time.

1. Who we are

StatusSkip ("we", "our", "us") is a software-as-a-service product that helps remote workers send weekly status report emails. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at [email protected].

2. What data we collect

When you use StatusSkip to send a report, we collect and log the following to a private Google Sheet for quota management and abuse prevention:

  • Your name (as entered in the form)
  • Your email address (optional — only if you request a confirmation copy)
  • Your manager's email address
  • The task text you entered (up to 3 tasks)
  • The progress values (0–100%) for each task
  • The timestamp of submission

We do not collect passwords, payment card details, precise location data, or any information beyond what you explicitly enter into the form.

3. How we use your data

We use the data you provide solely to:

  • Generate and deliver your HTML status report email to your manager
  • Send a confirmation copy to you (if you requested one)
  • Enforce the free plan's weekly usage limit
  • Detect and prevent abuse of the sending infrastructure
  • Send weekly reminder emails (Pro users only, and only if opted in)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service.

4. Data storage

Submission data is stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to the StatusSkip operator. It is not accessible to the public, and is not connected to any third-party advertising or analytics platform.

Google's infrastructure is subject to Google's own privacy and security standards. You can review Google's data processing terms at policies.google.com.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party, with the following narrow exceptions:

  • Delivery: Your manager's email address is used to deliver the report you requested. This is the core function of the service.
  • PayPal: If you upgrade to Pro, payment is processed by PayPal. We receive confirmation of payment and your PayPal email. We do not receive or store your card details.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose data if required by law or valid legal process.

6. Cookies and tracking

StatusSkip does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics scripts. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any similar tools. There is no cross-site tracking.

The only browser storage we use is localStorage to remember your free-tier usage count for the current week. This data never leaves your device.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Ask us to delete all data associated with your email address
  • Objection: Object to any processing you believe is unlawful

To exercise any of these rights, email [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data retention

We retain submission logs for up to 12 months for quota management purposes, after which they are deleted. If you request deletion before that period, we will delete your data within 14 days.

9. Children

StatusSkip is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe we have done so, contact us and we will delete the data immediately.
